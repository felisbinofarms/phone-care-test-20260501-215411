## What PhoneCare Does

PhoneCare is a phone maintenance app for iPhone. It helps you find duplicate photos and contacts, check storage usage, monitor battery health, and review privacy permissions. All processing happens on your device.

All plans include a 7-day free trial for new subscribers.

- You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
