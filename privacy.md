# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

PhoneCare is built to keep your information on your device.

## Summary

- We do not collect your personal data
- We do not run third-party analytics or ad SDKs
- We do not sell your data
- We do not upload your photos, contacts, or scan results to our servers

## 1. Data We Collect

PhoneCare does not collect personal information from your device to external servers.

The App stores necessary app state locally on your iPhone (for example, preferences and local scan results) to provide features.

## 2. Permissions We Request

PhoneCare may request access to:

- Photos (to detect duplicates/similar images)
- Contacts (to detect and merge duplicate contacts)
- Notifications (for optional reminders)

Permission access happens on-device and can be changed at any time in iOS Settings.

## 3. Subscription Processing

Purchases are processed by Apple using StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card information.

Apple may process purchase-related data according to Apple policies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties because PhoneCare does not collect user personal data on external servers.

## 5. Data Retention

Because data processing is local, data remains on your device unless you remove it, uninstall the App, or clear device data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

PhoneCare is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Security

We follow platform best practices and keep processing local to reduce privacy risk. No method is 100% secure, but minimizing data collection reduces exposure.

## 8. International Users

Because data is processed on your device, there is generally no cross-border transfer of your personal data by PhoneCare.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will update the effective date and provide notice where required.
